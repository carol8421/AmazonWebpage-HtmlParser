By 
S. George "ThingsWeUse" (USA) - See all my reviews
(REAL NAME)   
Verified Purchase(What's this?)
This review is from: Pampers Swaddlers Diaper Size 3 Giant Pack 124 Count (Health and Beauty)
We got the initial set of these from the hospital that our baby was born at. We got a box of huggies as a baby-shower gift. But those seemed to leak more. This one has a nice yellow adhesive strip. It sticks even if you stick and re-stick while making adjustments. Pretty good during the the first months when you obsessively/compulsively check and recheck to make sure its not too tight for the baby and too loose for leaks.

Tips:
1. Make sure that the yellow adhesive strip is completely on the diaper. We went through a few diapers of smaller size. The yellow adhesive stip would touch the baby''s skin irritating her. My wife found that it was slow when she saw some videos on how to use it.
2. I would suggest that check the diaper every hour or two even if the indicator has not changed color. Sometimes, the baby may have just pooped and I don't believe it changes color. I found a couple of occasions when the color did not change but the baby had pooped. Pee consistently changes the color of the indicator.

Pros:
1. Nice comfortable fit.
2. Yellow stripe changes to blue if the baby has peed.
3. Minimal leaks
4. Absolutely no rashes due to the diaper itself.

Cons:
1. Leaks from behind if the baby is having loose stools. I am not sure if this can be prevented. It just seems to be physics that when the baby poops wile sitting, it does not have anywhere to go. So it rises and leaks from behind.
