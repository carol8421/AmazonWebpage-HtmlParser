4.0 out of 5 stars I'd buy it again, but not online, October 20, 2014
By 
E. Dean (Maryland, USA) - See all my reviews
Verified Purchase(What's this?)
This review is from: Ball 4-Ounce Quilted Crystal Jelly Jars with Lids and Bands, Set of 12 (Kitchen)
I bought these about 6 months ago (I think), and have used them for homemade beauty products.

Two of the jars were unusable (manufacturer defect- glass shards/string protruding in the middle of the jar). The ones that were useable were great and I have reused them several times while with great delight.

My big problem is the price. I wasn't really able to get out of the house and shop around when I first bought these, but looking at the price now that I have, I am surprised. You can get them elsewhere (at large stores, in the U.S.) for about $10/dozen. The price online seems really high, especially since 2 of them weren't useable. I guess the defect isn't super unusual, because I got 2 defective jars from another (Ball brand) set I bought at Wal-Mart; but for a much lower price, it's easier to swallow the loss.

I would buy these jars again. They're great for gifting all kinds of things, like body butter, jellies, sauces, rubs, etc. But I would drive to a store for this purchase, I think it's over priced online.

5.0 out of 5 stars nice presenation jars for gifts, August 20, 2014
By 
Home is where they send us - See all my reviews
these are my "go to" canning jars for gifts. They are small enough for things like jellys and spreads and great for homemade scrubs or other crafts needing a nice gift container. They are also nice if you want to make and give several items at once but dont want to give a whole pint, for instance i make up several flavors of jams and pkg them together in these jars as a gift set.

Another reviewer mentioned that hers did not come with a cardboard grid that ball places between their jars to help cushion them and protect from breaking. All the ones I have purchased have come with them in the pkg. so im not sure if this is something new or something they have fixed.

the jars have a quilted look to them which really enhances presentation. although they are quite small they are very sturdy and have held up well to several cannings over the yrs. They use the regular fit tops which Ball sells seperately but they are a pretty standard size so other brands would fit as well.
